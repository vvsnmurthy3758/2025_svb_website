Our Nursery: History and Development

Sri Veerabhadra Nursery and Gardens has a rich history rooted in the fertile lands of Kadiyam, a region renowned for its horticultural excellence. Established over a decade ago, our nursery began with a vision to bring the beauty and benefits of nature closer to every home and landscape.

From humble beginnings, we have grown steadily, driven by a passion for plants and a commitment to quality. We've expanded our collection to include a diverse range of ornamental, fruit, flowering, indoor, and landscaping plants, catering to both individual enthusiasts and large-scale projects.

Our development has been marked by continuous learning, adoption of sustainable practices, and a focus on customer satisfaction. We pride ourselves on providing expert guidance, healthy plants, and personalized service, ensuring that every customer finds the perfect green addition to their space. We look forward to many more years of growing together with our community.

Our dedicated team of horticulturists and gardening enthusiasts are the backbone of our success. Their expertise and passion ensure that every plant nurtured here is healthy and ready to thrive in its new home.

We continuously invest in research and development to bring you the latest and most resilient plant varieties. Our commitment to innovation helps us stay at the forefront of the nursery industry.
